Introducing BlokNet

BlokNet helps families, schools, and offices create a safer browsing environment on shared Windows devices. It filters inappropriate websites and distracting platforms using domain and keyword-based controls, including access attempts made through VPN or proxy connections.

⭐ Key Features

Smart, powerful tools designed to keep your PC and family safe online:

  • Website Filtering by Domain — block unwanted or inappropriate websites using a maintained domain database.
  • Keyword-Based Filtering — help limit access to pages containing restricted or inappropriate terms.
  • Distraction Control — reduce access to selected social media, dating, or time-wasting platforms.
  • Custom Block List Support — Add your own sites for tailored protection based on your needs.
  • VPN & Proxy-Aware Filtering — helps enforce filtering even through VPN or proxy connections.
  • Browser Compatibility — Works on Chrome, Firefox, Edge, Brave, and more for full coverage.
  • User-Friendly Interface — Easy for beginners, powerful options for advanced users.

💡 Benefits

  • Safe browsing for children — prevent access to adult and inappropriate sites in real time.
  • Works across major browsers — supports Chrome, Firefox, Edge, Brave, and more.
  • VPN-aware protection — blocks inappropriate content even when accessed through VPN or proxy connections.
  • Custom site blocking — add specific websites based on your own needs.
  • Peace of mind — helps create a safer, focused online environment for families, students, and employees.

🖥️ System Requirements

  • OS: Windows 10 or 11
  • Dual-core CPU or better (Intel Core i3/AMD equivalent or newer)
  • Memory: 4 GB RAM or more recommended

❓ Frequently Asked Questions

  • Q: Can this filter sites accessed through a VPN? — BlokNet is designed to apply domain and keyword-based filtering even when access is attempted through VPN or proxy connections.
  • Can I add my own sites to block? — Absolutely! Just add domains to your custom block list anytime.
  • How do I bring the application back after hiding it? — Use the hotkeys: first try Ctrl + Alt + P. If that doesn’t work, use Ctrl + Shift + P.
