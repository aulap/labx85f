About Aulap Software

Aulap Software is an Indonesia-based software and cybersecurity initiative by Fajar Anggiawan. We build practical Windows utilities and provide digital security services for individuals, UMKM, and small organizations.

What We Build

Our software focuses on lightweight Windows security, safe browsing, productivity, monitoring, and everyday digital workflows.

Our Approach

We prioritize practical protection, clear communication, transparent downloads, responsible use, and support that is understandable for non-technical users.

Services in Indonesia

We provide incident response, system checkup, website security scan, and cybersecurity consulting for customers in Indonesia.
